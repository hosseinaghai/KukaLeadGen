⚠️ The Data Challenge

KUKA operates across 9 CRM and ERP systems spanning multiple business units, geographies, and entity models. Different ERPs serve different legal entities. Customer master data is fragmented, duplicated, and inconsistent — data quality is the critical bottleneck for any AI-driven lead scoring or account intelligence.

The Orchestration Layer solves this by normalising entity data from SAP, Salesforce, Dynamics, Oracle, and legacy systems into a single unified customer view. The Lead-Gen Agent reads from this layer — not from 9 individual systems. This is what makes automated prospect-to-customer matching possible at scale.
